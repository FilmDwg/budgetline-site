Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28/03/2025

1. Introduction

BudgetLine is a Google Sheets add-on designed to help users manage film budgets efficiently. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we access, use, store, and protect your data.

2. Data Collection & Usage

BudgetLine only accesses your Google Sheets data for functional purposes. Specifically, we use the following Google services:

Permissions & Their Purpose

  • Google Sheets (spreadsheets.currentonly) – Allows BudgetLine to read and modify the active spreadsheet where the add-on is installed.
  • Google Drive (drive.file) – Enables the add-on to create and update budget-related files within the user’s Drive. BudgetLine only interacts with files the user specifically selects.
  • Google Script UI (script.container.ui) – Displays sidebar menus for a better user experience.
  • Google User Info (userinfo.email, userinfo.profile) – Used to verify your Google Workspace account and check your license status.
  • External HTTP Requests (script.external_request) – Allows BudgetLine to make external HTTP requests to validate the user’s license.

BudgetLine does not access, store, or share your personal data outside of Google Workspace.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

BudgetLine does not share, transfer, or disclose user data to any third party. Your data remains within Google Workspace and is only used for the intended functionality of the add-on.

4. Data Protection & Security

We follow industry best practices to protect your data from unauthorized access. BudgetLine does not store any user information externally. All data remains within your Google account, protected by Google’s security infrastructure.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • No external storage – BudgetLine does not store any personal data outside of Google’s environment.
  • Data remains in your control – Any information created or modified by BudgetLine is stored only in your Google Sheets and Drive, where you can edit or delete it at any time.
  • Uninstalling the add-on – If you uninstall BudgetLine, it will no longer have access to your data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected here, and users will be notified through the add-on’s interface if necessary.
